ZETIC Melange device support and NPU acceleration.

ZETIC Melange broadly supports mobile devices across Android and iOS. We are continuously expanding device and NPU support.

NPU Acceleration
Melange automatically leverages NPU hardware when available for faster inference. Supported NPU platforms include:

Qualcomm Snapdragon (Hexagon HTP/DSP)
Apple Neural Engine
Google Tensor (Enterprise)
MediaTek Dimensity (Enterprise)
Samsung Exynos (Enterprise)
Google Tensor, MediaTek, and Samsung Exynos NPU support are available for enterprise customers. Contact us for access.

GPU and CPU Fallback
When NPU hardware is not available, Melange automatically falls back to GPU or CPU execution on both Android and iOS. Your app will work correctly on any device meeting the minimum requirements — NPU simply provides the best performance.

Minimum Requirements
Android
Minimum SDK 24 (Android 7.0)
Physical device (emulators do not have NPU hardware)
iOS
iOS 15.0+
Physical device required (simulators do not have Neural Engine)
We are actively expanding support to additional platforms and chipsets. If you need support for a specific device, contact us.
